111AR  Meaning of disclosing entity provisions

(1) The disclosing entity provisions are the provisions of the 
following:
(a) Chapter 2M as it applies to disclosing entities;
(d) sections 674, 674A, 675 and 675A;
(e) Division 4 of Part 8B.4 as it applies to disclosing entities;
(f) Division 2 of Part 8B.7 as it applies to disclosing entities.
