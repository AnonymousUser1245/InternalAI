Division 1 of Part 2M.3.

Requirements for report
(4) The auditor’s report must include any statements or disclosures 
required by the auditing standards for the purposes of this section.

(5) The auditor’s report must specify the date on which it is made.

Offences
(6) An offence based on subsection (2) is an offence of strict liability.

Note: For strict liability, see section 6.1 of the Criminal Code.
