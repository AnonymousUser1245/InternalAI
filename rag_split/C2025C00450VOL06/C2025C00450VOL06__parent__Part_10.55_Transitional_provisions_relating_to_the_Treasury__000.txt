Part 10.55  Transitional provisions relating to the Treasury Laws Amendment (Your
1683  Definitions

In this Part:
amending Act means the Treasury Laws Amendment (2021 
Measures No. 1) Act 2021.

Part 10.55  Transitional provisions relating to the Treasury Laws Amendment (Your
1683A  Application

The amendments made by Parts 1 and 2 of Schedule 2 to the 
amending Act to apply in relation to conduct that is engaged in on 
or after the commencement of those Parts.

Part 10.55  Transitional provisions relating to the Treasury Laws Amendment (Your
1683B  Review of operation of laws

(1) The Minister must cause a review of the operation of the 
amendments made by Parts 1 and 2 of Schedule 2 to the amending 
Act to be conducted by an independent expert within 6 months 
after the second anniversary of the commencement of this section.
(2) The person who conducts the review must give the Minister a 
written report of the review.
(3) The Minister must cause a copy of the report to be tabled in each 
House of the Parliament within 15 sitting days of that House after 
the report is given to the Minister.
Recommendations
(4) The report may set out recommendations to the Commonwealth 
Government.
