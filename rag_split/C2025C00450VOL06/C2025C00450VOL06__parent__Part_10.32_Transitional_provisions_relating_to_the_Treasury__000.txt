Part 10.32  Transitional provisions relating to the Treasury Laws Amendment
1644A  Application of amendments relating to penalties

The amendments made by Part 4 of Schedule 1 to the Treasury 
Laws Amendment (Enhancing Whistleblower Protections) Act 2019 
apply in relation to the commission of an offence if the conduct 
constituting the commission of the offence occurs wholly on or 
after the commencement of that Part.

1646  Decisions to give, withdraw or not withdraw a notice under

subsection 1313(1)

Corporations Amendment (Asia Region Funds Passport) Act 2018
