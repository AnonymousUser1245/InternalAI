Part 10.37  Transitional provisions relating to the Treasury Laws Amendment
1661  Application of amendments

(1) The amendments of section 588H by Schedule 1 to the Treasury 
Laws Amendment (Combating Illegal Phoenixing) Act 2020 apply 
in relation to debts incurred, and dispositions made, after the 
commencement of those amendments.
(2) Sections 203AA and 203AB, as inserted by Schedule 2 to the 
Treasury Laws Amendment (Combating Illegal Phoenixing) Act

Part 10.37  Transitional provisions relating to the Treasury Laws Amendment
2020, apply in relation to a person’s resignation as a director of a

company if the person stopped being a director of the company on 
or after the day that is 12 months after the day those sections 
commence.
(3) Section 203CA, as inserted by Schedule 2 to the Treasury Laws 
Amendment (Combating Illegal Phoenixing) Act 2020, applies in 
relation to a resolution that is to take effect on or after the day that 
is 12 months after the day that section commences.
