Part 10.75
Subdivision B—Widely held market bodies
1705H  Transitional—widely held market bodies

(1) A reference in paragraph 850A(2)(b) to a body corporate declared 
under subsection 850A(3) is taken to include a reference to a body 
corporate that, immediately before the commencement time, was 
specified for the purposes of paragraph 850A(b).
(2) Subsection (1) of this section ceases to have effect on the 
commencement of the first declaration made under 
subsection 850A(3).
