Part 7.1  Preliminary
Division 1—Preliminary
790A  Meaning of clearing and settlement arrangements

Clearing and settlement arrangements for a transaction effected 
through a financial market are arrangements for the clearing and 
settlement of those transactions.

The arrangements may be part of 
the market’s operating rules or be separate from those operating 
rules.
