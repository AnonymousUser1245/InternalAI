Compilation No. 143 Compilation date: 09/07/2025766H  Meaning of superannuation trustee service
(1) A person provides a superannuation trustee service if the person 
operates a registrable superannuation entity as trustee of the entity.
Note 1: The meaning of person here is affected by section 761FA (about 
multiple trustees).
Note 2: To determine whether a person to whom the service is provided is a 
retail client, see subsection 761G(6).
(2) However, the following do not constitute the provision of a 
superannuation trustee service:
(a) the operation of an exempt public sector superannuation 
scheme;
(b) conduct of a kind prescribed by regulations made for the 
purposes of this paragraph.
