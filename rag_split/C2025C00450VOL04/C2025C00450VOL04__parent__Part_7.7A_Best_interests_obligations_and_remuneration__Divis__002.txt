Division 4  Conflicted remuneration
963P  Person covered by section 963M must pay amount etc. in

accordance with regulations
If regulations made for the purposes of subsection 963N(1) require 
a person covered by section 963M to pay an amount, or provide a 
monetary benefit, the person must pay the amount or provide the 
monetary benefit in accordance with those regulations.
Note: This section is a civil penalty provision (see section 1317E).
