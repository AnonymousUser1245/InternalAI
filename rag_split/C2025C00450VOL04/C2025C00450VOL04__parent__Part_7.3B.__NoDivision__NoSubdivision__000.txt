Part 7.3B.
821D  Obligation to give access to the facility

(1) A CS facility licensee must give a person authorised by ASIC such 
reasonable access to the facility as the person requests for any of 
the purposes of this Chapter.
Note: Failure to comply with this subsection is an offence (see 
subsection 1311(1)).
(2) A CS facility licensee must give a person authorised by the 
Reserve Bank such reasonable access to the facility as the person 
requests for any of the purposes of this Part or Part 7.3B.
Note: Failure to comply with this subsection is an offence: see 
subsection 1311(1).

Part 7.3B.
821E  Annual report

(1) A CS facility licensee must, within 3 months after the end of its 
financial year, give ASIC an annual report on the extent to which 
the licensee complied with its obligations as a CS facility licensee 
under this Chapter.
Note: Failure to comply with this subsection is an offence (see 
subsection 1311(1)).
